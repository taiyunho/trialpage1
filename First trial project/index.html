History and Debut

EXID officially made their debut in 2012 with 6 members (Yuzi, Dami, Haeryeong, Hani, Jeonghwa and LE). The group debuted with the track "Whoz That Girl".
However, later that year, Yuzi, Dami and Haeryeong left the group. Solji and Hyelin replaced the 3 and formed the current EXID. The group continued to promote with tracks such as "I Feel Good"and "Every Night".
They enjoyed some success but shot to fame when a fancam of Hani dancing to their track "Up and Down" became viral on the Internet. From then on, they went on to release upbeat and popular tracks such as "Hot Pink" and "Ah Yeah", which brought them even more fame and popularity. EXID came back with their title track "L.I.E.", which was also composed and produced by LE, who is the rapper of the group. The song won them Number Ones on music shows, crediting the hard work that LE and the group had put in. However, in December 2016, Solji was diagnosed with Hyperthyroidism and went on a hiatus.
The group continued promoting as a 4 member group and came back with more songs such as "Night Rather Than Night", "DDD". Solji returned vocally in "DDD". The group returned with "Lady" in April 2018 and finally came back as a full group with Solji with "I Love You" in the same year. The group continued to enjoy success until in 2019, the group ended their contracts with their company, with Hani, Jeonghwa and Hyelin leaving the group first. Subsequently, Solji and LE left the company.
However, the group still desires to promote and release new tracks in the future. The group shares a strong bond wih one another. Hoping to hear some good news from EXID!


Discography and Music Videos

Members of EXID

Hani

Hani was a former trainee of JYP before joining EXID. She is also part of the subunit Dasoni with member Solji. Hani co-hosted some variety shows such as "A Style for You" and "Weekly Idol". Funny and comical, Hani has also brought laughter and smiles to audience's faces when she appeared on other variety shows such as Running Man. Just like all her members, Hani appeared on "King of Masked Singer".

Hyelin

Hyelin, like Solji, joined EXID when the previous 3 members left the group. Being one of the lead vocalist of EXID, Hyelin has also joined her members and competed on "King of Masker Singer". Hyelin has showcased her talent in many areas, one of it being a look-alike with Voldemort. Hyelin is also known to be quite financially savvy.

Jeong Hwa

Jeonghwa was part of the original line up of EXID. Jeonghwa was originally set up to be one of the rappers of EXID. However, throughout the years with subsequent comebacks, Jeonghwa has taken up more sub vocalist roles. She is the Maknae of the group. Jeonghwa is also a good dancer and actress.

Solji

Solji was a former member of the group 2NB. Solji released many singles under 2NB but not all of them brought her fame. Solji wass EXID's vocal trainer before joining the group. In 2015, Solji won on the variety singing show called "King of Masked Singer". Solji was also a member of Dasoni with her fellow EXID member Hani. in December 2016, Solji was diagnosed with Hyperthyroidism and went on a hiatus. However, the group came back with Solji after her recovery, releasing several tracks. Solji has even participated in Masked Singer again after her recovery.

L.E.

L.E. is the rapper of EXID. She was part of the underground group Jiggy Fellaz before joining EXID. In 2016, EXID released their debut studio album with the title track L.I.E. The song won the group several first places. The album was partly produced, with lyrics partly composed by L.E. Apart from having a unique voice that makes her an outstanding rapper in the industry, L.E. demonstrated her talent in producing music as exemplified in her lyrical contributions in many songs for EXID.
